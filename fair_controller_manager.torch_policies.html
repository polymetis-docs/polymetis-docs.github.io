fair_controller_manager.torch_policies package¶

Policies for robot control in PyTorch.

Note: this module will be deprecated in favor of torchcontrol once that is stable. In the meantime, the default functionalities provided by RobotInterface inherit from ScriptableTorchPolicy here.

fair_controller_manager.torch_policies.op_space_controller module¶

Created on Nov 04 13:00:00 2019

@author: gsutanto @comment: Velocity-based Operational Space Controller, as described in

the IJRR paper “Operational Space Control: A Theoretical and Empirical Comparison” (2008) by Nakanishi et al., section 3.1.2 (equation (23)), with additional proportional (P) term, instead of just derivative (D) term. Input parameters: desired end-effector (goal) pose Outputs: torque (combination of both feed-forward term & feedback term) that minimizes end-effector pose error
