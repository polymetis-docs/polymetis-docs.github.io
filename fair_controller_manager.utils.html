class fair_controller_manager.utils.planner.OrientationPlanner(goal, goal_vel, goal_acc)¶

Bases: fair_controller_manager.utils.planner.DifferentialEquationPlanner

Orientation motion planner.

Similar to DifferentialEquationPlanner, but for 3-dimensional Special Orthogonal group (SO(3) group), which is a representation of orientation in 3D space. In other words, this is useful for orientation motion planning.
